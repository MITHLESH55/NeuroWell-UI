🔐 Introduction

NeuroWell ("we", "our", or "us") operates the NeuroWell wellness intelligence platform. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve our service. By using NeuroWell, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

📊 Data We Collect

We collect different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service:

  • Assessment Data: Your responses to wellness assessment questions including physical, mental, and emotional health information
  • Score Data: Calculated wellness scores, trends, and predictive forecasts
  • Account Information: Email, name, and password (if you create an account)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform (page views, time spent, features used)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address
  • Contact Information: If you contact us directly, we collect the information you provide

💡 How We Use Your Data

NeuroWell uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To calculate wellness scores and generate predictions
  • To provide personalized recommendations
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To allow you to participate in interactive features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analysis and analytics to improve our service
  • To monitor the usage of our service
  • To detect, prevent and address fraud and other technical issues

🛡️ Data Protection & Security

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We implement the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Compliance with GDPR and international data protection standards
  • Local storage options for users who prefer offline access

⚖️ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
